About

DAVID DRAKE

David has been working trades in North Florida since 1999 — starting as a journeyman electrician and expanding into general contracting, plumbing, carpentry, and everything in between.

FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

How do I get a free estimate?▾

Fill out the estimate form on this page or call (386) 365-9221. David will come out to take a look at no charge and give you a straight answer on cost.

What areas do you serve?▾

Suwannee, Columbia, Hamilton, Lafayette, Madison, Dixie, Gilchrist and Levy counties. Not on the list? Call — we may still cover you.

How fast can David get to my job?▾

Response time varies by season, but David typically reaches out same-day and can often schedule an estimate visit within a few days.

What payment methods are accepted?▾

Cash, check, CashApp, Zelle, and digital assets are all accepted. No credit card surcharges.

Do you handle both residential and commercial jobs?▾

Yes — David works on homes, rental properties, farms, small businesses and commercial buildings throughout North Florida.
